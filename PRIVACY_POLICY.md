# Privacy Policy for STRIPED Golf Training

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

## Introduction

STRIPED ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the STRIPED mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password (securely stored)
- **Profile Information**: Golf skill level, practice goals, facility access, practice time availability
- **Training Data**: Session completions, drill performance, skill scores, practice notes

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Session timestamps, drill completion rates, app feature usage
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility and debugging)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect your location data
- We do NOT use tracking cookies or analytics
- We do NOT collect payment information (app is currently free)
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the STRIPED experience:

1. **Account Management**: Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
2. **Training Programs**: Deliver personalized training sessions based on your skill level
3. **Progress Tracking**: Store and display your practice history and skill improvements
4. **App Improvement**: Identify bugs and improve app functionality

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: All data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States
- **Encryption**: 
  - Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  - Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  - Data at rest is encrypted in our database (DynamoDB)
- **Access Control**: Only you can access your training data through authenticated API requests

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.** Period.

The only exception is if required by law (e.g., valid court order or legal process).

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your personal data through the app's Profile screen
- **Edit**: Update your profile information, skill level, and goals at any time
- **Delete**: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format (JSON)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: jacob@smallstack.co

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: When you delete your account, we permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days
- **Backup Retention**: Backup copies may exist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes

## Children's Privacy

STRIPED is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying an in-app notification when you next open the app

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
